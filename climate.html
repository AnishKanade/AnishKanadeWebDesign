Climate

Alaska

How has it already been impacted?

The State’s Ice caps are melting in remarkable rate. Wildfires have destroyed many forests in Alaska. Many places in Alaska are now experiencing dry lands. There is now only half as much sea ice then before.

How it will be impacted in the future:

If Climate change continue affecting Alaska, then there is a high chance that the ice glaciers will disappear in the future. There would be long summers and less winter. More large wildfires would destroy more forests in Alaska.

What can be done to minimize the impact of climate change:

  • Use energy wisely and not waste natural resources.
  • Use green commute.
  • Invest in renewables.
  • Decrease pollution.

What are the people in the area already doing to limit the impact of climate change:

  • More people are trying to commute green.
  • Many people are raising awareness.
  • Many people are cleaning rivers and oceans.
  • People are reducing carbon emissions.
